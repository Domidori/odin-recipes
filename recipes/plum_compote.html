Syrniki recipe

Description

Here’s a simple recipe for plum-compote (Russian fuit lemonade):

  • 500g plums, halved and pitted
  • 1.5 liters of water
  • 150g sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 1-2 cloves (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add sugar, stirring until fully dissolved.
  2. Add the plums, cinnamon stick, and cloves to the boiling water.
  3. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10–15 minutes, until the plums are tender but not falling apart.
  4. Remove from heat and let the compote cool.
  5. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Enjoy these traditional dishes!
